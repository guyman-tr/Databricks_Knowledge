## 1. Business Meaning

GetCustomerFinanceDetails retrieves a customer's aggregated financial metrics from the CustomerAggregatedData table. The TotalCommission value (sum of commissions on open and close) and the last position timestamps tell the commission engine whether the customer has met the trading activity thresholds required for certain compensation models (e.g., CPAD - CPA with deposit - requires the customer to have traded).

This procedure exists as a lightweight lookup in the commission calculation pipeline. Rather than scanning all positions for a customer, it reads the pre-computed aggregates. The data is used to determine if an affiliate qualifies for deposit-based commissions (CPAD model) based on the referred customer's trading activity.

---

## 2. Business Logic

### 2.1 Total Commission Calculation

**What**: Combines open-position and close-position commissions into a single total.

**Columns/Parameters Involved**: `OpenedPositionsCommissionOnOpen`, `TotalCommissionOnClose`, `TotalCommission`

**Rules**:
- TotalCommission = ISNULL(OpenedPositionsCommissionOnOpen, 0) + ISNULL(TotalCommissionOnClose, 0)
- Both source columns can be NULL (customer has no positions yet), so ISNULL defaults to 0
- This total represents the lifetime commission generated by this customer's trading activity

## 8. Sample Queries

### 8.1 Get finance details for customer 12345
```sql
EXEC [AffiliateCommission].[GetCustomerFinanceDetails] @CID = 12345
```

### 8.2 Find customers with highest total commissions
```sql
SELECT TOP 20 CID,
       ISNULL(OpenedPositionsCommissionOnOpen, 0) + ISNULL(TotalCommissionOnClose, 0) AS TotalCommission,
       LastOpenedPosition, LastClosedPosition
FROM [AffiliateCommission].[CustomerAggregatedData] WITH (NOLOCK)
ORDER BY TotalCommission DESC
```

### 8.3 Find customers who have deposited but never traded
```sql
SELECT CID, LastOpenedPosition, LastClosedPosition
FROM [AffiliateCommission].[CustomerAggregatedData] WITH (NOLOCK)
WHERE LastOpenedPosition IS NULL
    AND LastClosedPosition IS NULL
```
