## 1. Business Meaning

dbo.tblaff_Sales_Commissions records affiliate commissions from sales/spread revenue events (tblaff_Sales). A "sale" in the affiliate context represents trading spread revenue generated by a referred customer. This is the second-largest commission table (~3.08M records), as affiliates often earn ongoing spread-based commissions for every trade their customers make.

Triggers enforce RI against tblaff_Affiliates and tblaff_Sales. The unique `UsedBonusCommission` column tracks how much of the commission was offset by bonus usage, enabling accurate net commission calculations.

---

## 2. Business Logic

### 2.1 Multi-Tier Sales Commission

**Columns/Parameters Involved**: `SalesID`, `AffiliateID`, `Commission`, `Tier`, `UsedBonusCommission`

**Rules**:
- SalesID references tblaff_Sales. Triggers enforce RI.
- Standard multi-tier: Tier 1 = direct, 2-5 = parents
- `UsedBonusCommission` (decimal(18,6)) tracks the portion of commission offset by bonus usage - unique to this commission table
- Net commission = Commission - UsedBonusCommission

### 2.2 Payment Lifecycle

- Same pattern: Paid=0/PaymentID=0 = unpaid, Paid=1/PaymentID>0 = paid

## 8. Sample Queries

### 8.1 Unpaid sales commissions by affiliate (net of bonus)
```sql
SELECT AffiliateID, Tier, COUNT(*) AS Records,
       SUM(Commission) AS GrossCommission,
       SUM(ISNULL(UsedBonusCommission, 0)) AS BonusOffset,
       SUM(Commission) - SUM(ISNULL(UsedBonusCommission, 0)) AS NetCommission
FROM dbo.tblaff_Sales_Commissions WITH (NOLOCK)
WHERE Paid = 0
GROUP BY AffiliateID, Tier ORDER BY NetCommission DESC
```

### 8.2 Sales with bonus offset analysis
```sql
SELECT AffiliateID, COUNT(*) AS TotalSales,
       SUM(CASE WHEN UsedBonusCommission > 0 THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) AS BonusAffectedSales,
       SUM(UsedBonusCommission) AS TotalBonusOffset
FROM dbo.tblaff_Sales_Commissions WITH (NOLOCK)
WHERE UsedBonusCommission IS NOT NULL
GROUP BY AffiliateID ORDER BY TotalBonusOffset DESC
```

### 8.3 Sales commission with event details
```sql
SELECT sc.Commission, sc.Tier, sc.UsedBonusCommission, s.Optional3 AS CustomerCID
FROM dbo.tblaff_Sales_Commissions sc WITH (NOLOCK)
JOIN dbo.tblaff_Sales s WITH (NOLOCK) ON sc.SalesID = s.SalesID
WHERE sc.AffiliateID = @AffiliateID AND sc.Paid = 0
ORDER BY sc.ID DESC
```
