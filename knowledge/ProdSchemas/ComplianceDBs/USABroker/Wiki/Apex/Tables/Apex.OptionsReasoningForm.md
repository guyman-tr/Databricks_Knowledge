## 1. Business Meaning

Apex.OptionsReasoningForm records each instance of a reasoning form created when a customer wants to change their options trading suitability questionnaire answers. When a customer's appropriateness test result changes (usually after answering suitability questions differently), the system creates a reasoning form to capture why they changed their answers. This is a regulatory requirement to document customer intent when modifying suitability assessments.

This table exists to create an audit trail for suitability answer changes. Regulators may require evidence that answer changes were voluntary and not coached. The form captures when it was created, when (if) it was submitted, and what the previous appropriateness test date was - allowing comparison of before/after states.

Data is created by Apex.CreateOptionsReasoningFormQuestion, which inserts the form header (this table) and the individual question-answer pairs (OptionsReasoningFormQuestionsAnswers) in a single procedure call. The ReasoningFormID is generated by the application and passed in. The form starts with DateSubmitted=NULL and is updated when the customer completes and submits the form. Referenced by Apex.Options via the ReasoningFormID column.

## 2. Business Logic

### 2.1 Reasoning Form Lifecycle

**What**: Each form goes through creation (with NULL submit date) and optional submission, tracking the timeline of the customer's reasoning process.

**Columns/Parameters Involved**: `ReasoningFormID`, `GCID`, `DateCreated`, `DateSubmitted`, `PreviousAppropriatenessTestDate`

**Rules**:
- Form is created with DateCreated=GETUTCDATE() and DateSubmitted=NULL
- PreviousAppropriatenessTestDate captures the timestamp of the last suitability test before the change, enabling before/after comparison
- DateSubmitted is populated when the customer finalizes and submits the form
- A NULL DateSubmitted indicates an abandoned or in-progress form
- The form is idempotent: CreateOptionsReasoningFormQuestion checks for existing form before inserting (IF NOT EXISTS pattern)
- Multiple questions can be added to the same form via repeated calls to CreateOptionsReasoningFormQuestion

**Diagram**:
```
Customer changes suitability answers
    |
    v
CreateOptionsReasoningFormQuestion called
    |
    +-> OptionsReasoningForm INSERT (DateSubmitted=NULL)
    +-> OptionsReasoningFormQuestionsAnswers INSERT (per question)
    |
    v
Customer reviews reasoning form
    |
    v
Customer submits form -> DateSubmitted = UTC now
    |
    v
Options.ReasoningStatusID updated to PendingManualReview
```

## 8. Sample Queries

### 8.1 Get recent reasoning forms with submission status

```sql
SELECT ReasoningFormID, GCID, DateCreated, DateSubmitted,
       PreviousAppropriatenessTestDate,
       CASE WHEN DateSubmitted IS NULL THEN 'In Progress / Abandoned'
            ELSE 'Submitted' END AS FormStatus
FROM Apex.OptionsReasoningForm WITH (NOLOCK)
ORDER BY DateCreated DESC;
```

### 8.2 Find abandoned forms (created but never submitted)

```sql
SELECT ReasoningFormID, GCID, DateCreated,
       DATEDIFF(HOUR, DateCreated, GETUTCDATE()) AS HoursSinceCreation
FROM Apex.OptionsReasoningForm WITH (NOLOCK)
WHERE DateSubmitted IS NULL
  AND DATEDIFF(DAY, DateCreated, GETUTCDATE()) > 7
ORDER BY DateCreated ASC;
```

### 8.3 Get a specific form with its questions and answers

```sql
SELECT f.ReasoningFormID, f.GCID, f.DateCreated, f.DateSubmitted,
       qa.KycQuestionID, qa.OldKycAnswerID, qa.ReasoningFormAnswerID,
       rfa.AnswerText AS ReasoningAnswer
FROM Apex.OptionsReasoningForm f WITH (NOLOCK)
INNER JOIN Apex.OptionsReasoningFormQuestionsAnswers qa WITH (NOLOCK)
    ON qa.ReasoningFormID = f.ReasoningFormID
LEFT JOIN Dictionary.OptionsReasoningFormAnswers rfa WITH (NOLOCK)
    ON rfa.ReasoningFormAnswerID = qa.ReasoningFormAnswerID
WHERE f.GCID = 19200728
ORDER BY qa.KycQuestionID;
```
