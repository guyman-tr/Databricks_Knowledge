## 1. Business Meaning

History.CreditNotes stores short text descriptions for credit records. Each row links a credit ID (bigint, from the credit/bonus system) to a human-readable note explaining what the credit is for.

3,822 rows. CreditID values are in the high bigint range (~2.1 billion), consistent with the system's IDENTITY-generated credit IDs. The dominant note text is "Automation Compensation" - credits awarded through automated compensation processes (likely robotic/batch processing).

This table provides the descriptive label that back-office and reporting systems attach to credit entries when displaying credit history to users or agents.
