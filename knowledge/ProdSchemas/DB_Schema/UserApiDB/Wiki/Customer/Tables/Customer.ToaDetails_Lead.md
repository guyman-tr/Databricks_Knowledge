## 1. Business Meaning

Customer.ToaDetails_Lead captures pre-registration lead data for users transferring their accounts from Chinese partner platforms (TOA - Transfer of Account). Before the user fully registers on eToro, their identity details from the source platform are stored here. The ToaId is the external identifier from the partner, and MamcId is the converted internal identifier generated by Customer.ConvertToaIdToMamcId.

This table supports the China market onboarding flow where users migrate from a partner platform. Lead data is captured first, and upon successful registration, a corresponding row is created in Customer.ToaDetails_Registration. Masked columns (FullName, ToaPhone, ChineseIdNumber) protect PII through dynamic data masking.
