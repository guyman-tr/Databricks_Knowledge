# BI_DB_dbo.BI_DB_Crypto_Net_Units_End_Of_Month

## 1. Overview

Monthly snapshot of **net crypto units held at the end of a calendar month** per customer, regulation, and instrument. Captures positions that are still open at month end -- the actual crypto exposure at the close of the period. Used by the finance team for monthly crypto reconciliation.

**Row grain**: One CID + Regulation + Instrument + SettlementType per Month

---

## 2. Business Context

Part of the **Crypto RECON** trio (populated by `SP_M_Crypto_RECON`). Unlike `BI_DB_Crypto_Net_Units_During_Month` which captures flow, this table answers: "What crypto does the customer hold at month end?"

**Key business rules**:
- **Units formula**: `SUM(AmountInUnitsDecimal * (2*IsBuy - 1))` -- simple net of buys minus sells for positions still open.
- **Position filter**: `OpenDateID <= @endINT AND (CloseDateID > @endINT OR CloseDateID = 0)` -- open on or before month end, not yet closed.
- **Crypto only**: `InstrumentTypeID = 10`.
- **SettlementType**: Real / CFD / TRS / CMT (added Feb 2022).
- **IsValidCustomer**: Included as column, not filtered (since Mar 2022).

**Sibling tables**: `BI_DB_Crypto_Net_Units_During_Month` (during-month flow), `BI_DB_Crypto_Zero` (P&L reconciliation).
