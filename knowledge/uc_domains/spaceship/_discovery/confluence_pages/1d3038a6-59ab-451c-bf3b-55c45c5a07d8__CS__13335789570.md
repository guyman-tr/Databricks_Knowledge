# Spaceship (retirement funds in Australia) — Internal guidelines

## About Spaceship

Spaceship is a financial platform offering its own mutual funds, retirement funds and US-market share trading in Australia. eToro acquired Spaceship in **November 2024**.

Spaceship offers:

* **Spaceship Super** — a superannuation fund
* **Spaceship Voyager** — a suite of managed funds
* **US Investing** — the ability to invest in a selection of US-listed stocks and ETFs

Even though Spaceship is part of eToro Group, they operate under their own licence for certain services. eToro provides clients with their Financial Services Guide (FSG) to ensure clients have information about their financial services.

For more information visit [www.spaceship.com.au](https://www.spaceship.com.au/).

## Eligibility

We offer this to any client living in Australia (by IP address / KYC checks) and no problematic screening ID.

## Flow

### 1. Creating the account

When interacted with the promotional content:

1. Users with an eToro investment account verification level 3 will be redirected to Spaceship.
2. Users with an eToro investment account verification level 1 or 2 have to complete the verification inside eToro before they are redirected to Spaceship.

### 2. Linking a Spaceship account to eToro

After verifying the eToro account and ensuring personal details are up-to-date with both eToro and Spaceship:

1. Login to the eToro platform.
2. Select the Wallet tab.
3. Scroll down to the Spaceship content.
4. Select 'Link Spaceship Account'.
5. Follow the prompts.

Once linked, Spaceship Voyager, US Investing, and Available Money balances will appear in the **Wallet** tab in the eToro app.

Clients can unlink their Spaceship and eToro accounts by contacting Spaceship's customer support directly.

## Identifying clients with Spaceship accounts

To see if a user is a connected mutual client:

1. Go to Salesforce.
2. Go to the user's profile.
3. Select the 'details' tab.
4. Check the Connected accounts panel that contains information on whether the user is a mutual (connected) client or not, and the 'external ID' which connects the two accounts.

* If the user is connected: the panel will also contain information on the user's Spaceship products.
* If the user has a Spaceship account, but they haven't connected it with their eToro account: the user's Spaceship product information will not be shown in the panel. I.e. only connected customers will have their Spaceship product information populated in Salesforce.

## Costs

Spaceship's fees vary depending on the product you choose. For more information on Spaceship's products, visit [www.spaceship.com.au](https://www.spaceship.com.au/).

## Transferring holdings

Clients can transfer their Spaceship US Investing holdings to eToro and viceversa, provided the assets are available on both platforms. Fees and conditions are involved.

## Spaceship balance and eToro Club balance

Spaceship Voyager, US Investing and Available Money balances are counted towards eToro Club balance.

**Spaceship Super won't be included in the Club balance due to regulatory reasons.**

## Spaceship balance refresh cadence

> **Your Spaceship product balances in the eToro app are populated once a day by Spaceship. This means intra-day movements in your product balances may not be reflected in the eToro app in real time.**

Additionally, Spaceship balances in the Spaceship app are shown in AUD; check if the currency set in the eToro app is also in AUD.
